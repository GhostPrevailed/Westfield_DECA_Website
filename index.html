About Our Chapter

Westfield High School DECA is more than just a club — it's a community of driven students exploring business, leadership, and innovation. From national competitions to local fundraisers, we empower students to think critically, collaborate effectively, and make an impact.

With award-winning events, dedicated mentorship, and unforgettable experiences, our chapter continues to push boundaries and inspire the next generation of leaders.
