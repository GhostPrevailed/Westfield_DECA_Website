Shoe Fundraiser

The Shoe Collection fundraiser is ongoing until November 31st to support SLC. DECA earns 40 cents per pound of gently used or new shoes in good condition (no holes or damage). Drop-offs at Mrs. Gramann’s classroom (E207), the main office, or nearby schools: Oakton HS, Floris ES, Stone MS, Virginia Run ES, and Bull Run ES. Spread the word and contribute!

Calendar Fundraiser

The October calendar fundraiser wrapped up on October 31st. All proceeds from this fundraiser help cover our expenses for SLC. It’s crucial that all DECA members submit their calendar sheets as soon as possible. Thank you to everyone who contributed and participated!
